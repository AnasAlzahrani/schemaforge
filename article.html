How to Use Article Schema Markup

      

        

          
1

          
Enter Article Details

          
Fill in the headline, author, publisher, and dates. The headline should match your H1 tag.

        

        

          
2

          
Add Publisher Info

          
Include your organization name and logo. Google requires publisher information for article rich results.

        

        

          
3

          
Copy & Implement

          
Copy the JSON-LD code and paste it into the <head> of your article page.

        

        

          
4

          
Update on Changes

          
When you update the article, regenerate the schema with the new dateModified value.
