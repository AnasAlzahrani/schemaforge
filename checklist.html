Power & DAG Checks

Sample size justification + causal structure verification

Preview: What's Inside

  • Research question maps to a testable causal estimand
  • Study type matches the available variation (experimental vs natural)
  • DAG drawn with all measured and unmeasured confounders
  • Primary outcome defined with measurement validity evidence
  • Sample size justified with power calculation (not "convenience")
  • Allocation method prevents selection bias at assignment
  • Blinding strategy specified for outcome assessment
  • Pre-analysis plan registered or documented
  • Sensitivity analysis planned for key assumptions
  • + 14 more checks across internal & external validity
