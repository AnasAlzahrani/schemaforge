How to Use Organization Schema Markup

      

        

          
1

          
Enter Organization Info

          
Fill in your organization's name, website URL, logo, and description. These form the core of your knowledge panel.

        

        

          
2

          
Add Contact Details

          
Include your main contact point — phone, email, and the type of contact. Google uses this for direct contact features.

        

        

          
3

          
Link Social Profiles

          
Add your official social media URLs. These appear in Google's knowledge panel and help verify your brand.

        

        

          
4

          
Add to Your Homepage

          
Add the JSON-LD to your homepage or About page. Organization schema works best site-wide.
