## Tweet 1 (Hook)
Google killed their free schema markup tool.

SchemaApp put theirs behind a paywall.

So I built my own. It's free, it's fast, and it works. 🧵

---

## Tweet 2 (What it is)
ProtoCol generates valid JSON-LD structured data for 8 schema types:

→ FAQ Page
→ Local Business  
→ Product
→ Article
→ Event
→ How-To
→ Organization
→ Recipe

Pick a type, fill in the form, copy the code. No signup. No account. Nothing.

---

## Tweet 3 (How it works)
The JSON-LD generates in real-time as you type.

One click to copy. Paste it into your <head> tag. Test it with Google's Rich Results Test.

That's the entire workflow. Under 60 seconds.

---

## Tweet 4 (Tech angle)
Built with zero dependencies.

Pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JS. No React, no build step, no backend. Everything runs in your browser.

Loads instantly because there's genuinely nothing to load.

---

## Tweet 5 (Why free)
Why free? Because schema markup shouldn't be gated behind a subscription.

It's a form that generates JSON. The fact that people were charging for this always felt wrong.
